🏆 Finance Health Score

One number. Four pillars. Infinite motivation.

Your Finance Health Score is a 0–100 rating updated every time you log a transaction. It turns the abstract idea of "financial health" into something you can actually see improve over time.

  • Savings Rate (9/10) — what percentage of income are you keeping
  • Budget Adherence (7/10) — how well you're sticking to your limits
  • Spending Trend (8/10) — is your spending going up or down vs last month
  • Goal Progress (6.7/10) — are you on track to hit your financial goals
  • Large animated gradient ring — green to blue as score rises
  • FlowAI tip card with one specific action to improve your score
  • Score history tracked month-over-month to show your progress

🔒 Security

Your financial data stays private and locked.

Finance Flow never asks for your bank credentials. All data is stored locally and encrypted. Multiple security layers — from biometric unlock to auto-lock and screenshot blocking — keep your information yours alone.

  • Biometric Login — Face ID and fingerprint unlock on iOS and Android
  • Change Password — update your account credentials anytime
  • Two-Factor Authentication — SMS or authenticator app for login
  • Auto Lock — app locks after configurable inactivity period
  • Screenshot Lock — prevents screen captures of sensitive screens
  • All data encrypted at rest with AES-256
  • Danger Zone: Delete all data or delete account — permanent and instant

📦 Data & Privacy

Your data is yours. Completely, always.

Finance Flow is built on a simple promise: we never sell your data, never train AI models on your transactions, and give you full control to export or delete everything — immediately, no questions asked.

  • Export everything as PDF, Excel, or CSV — any time, instantly
  • Cloud backup with automatic daily sync and timestamp confirmation
  • Delete all your data permanently in one tap from the app
  • Delete account — full removal from all servers within 24 hours
  • FlowAI runs on per-session context — never stored, never logged
  • Zero third-party analytics SDKs embedded in the app
  • Privacy Policy, Terms, and Security Policy accessible in-app
